About

Kailani Makoa is a Native Hawaiian healer and practitioner of lomilomi, the traditional Hawaiian art of massage. With over 15 years of experience, she integrates ancient Hawaiian healing practices with modern wellness approaches. Her practice focuses on restoring balance and harmony through the connection of body, mind, and spirit. Each session is customized to address individual needs, incorporating breathwork, energy healing, and traditional Hawaiian prayer (pule). Kailani trained under master practitioners on the Big Island and continues to deepen her practice through ongoing study of Hawaiian healing traditions.

About Leilani

I grew up watching my grandmother practice Lomilomi on the shores of Maui, learning the rhythms and prayers that have been part of our family for three generations. My practice is rooted in Hawaiian healing arts, blending traditional Lomilomi with deep tissue techniques refined over years of study.

Today I work from a private studio in Lahaina, where the ocean is always near and the aloha spirit guides every session.
